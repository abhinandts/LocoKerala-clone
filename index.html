What is LocoKerala?



LocoKerala is a distinguished organic and natural products company hailing from Kerala. We specialize in crafting exquisite organic teas, coffees, and spice products using traditional farming practices in our organic and rainforest plantations located in Idukki, Wayanad, and Nilgiris. Our commitment to natural cultivation methods is deeply rooted in ancient Indian agricultural wisdom, ensuring that our products are nurtured in the most authentic way possible.

We take great pride in being associated with a group of small farmers from Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Assam, and Darjeeling who have transitioned to organic cultivation and now produce some of the finest organic products in the region. By collaborating with these passionate farmers, we bring their exceptional handmade and homegrown organic and natural products directly to customers around the world.

At LocoKerala, we strive to create a direct connection between customers and small farmers, ensuring fair prices for their meticulously crafted organic offerings. By supporting us, you not only enjoy the incredible flavors and benefits of our products but also contribute to the livelihoods and sustainability of these dedicated farmers. Experience the true essence of organic and natural goodness with LocoKerala.
